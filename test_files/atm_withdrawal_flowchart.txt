ATM WITHDRAWAL PROCESS FLOWCHART
=================================

PROCESS: Customer withdraws money from ATM machine

FLOWCHART DESCRIPTION:
---------------------

START
  ↓
[Insert Card]
  ↓
<Is Card Valid?> ——NO——→ [Display "Invalid Card"] ——→ [Return Card] ——→ END
  ↓ YES
[Enter PIN]
  ↓
<Is PIN Correct?> ——NO——→ [Increment Attempt Counter]
  ↓ YES                    ↓
[Display Main Menu]        <Attempts < 3?> ——YES——→ [Display "Try Again"] ——→ [Enter PIN]
  ↓                        ↓ NO
[Select Withdrawal]        [Display "Card Blocked"] ——→ [Retain Card] ——→ END
  ↓
[Enter Amount]
  ↓
<Amount ≤ Account Balance?> ——NO——→ [Display "Insufficient Funds"] ——→ [Display Main Menu]
  ↓ YES
<Amount ≤ ATM Cash Available?> ——NO——→ [Display "ATM Out of Cash"] ——→ [Display Main Menu]
  ↓ YES
<Amount ≤ Daily Limit?> ——NO——→ [Display "Exceeds Daily Limit"] ——→ [Display Main Menu]
  ↓ YES
[Process Transaction]
  ↓
[Update Account Balance]
  ↓
[Dispense Cash]
  ↓
[Update ATM Cash Count]
  ↓
[Print Receipt?] ——YES——→ [Print Receipt]
  ↓ NO               ↓
[Display "Thank You"] ←——————————————
  ↓
[Return Card]
  ↓
END

SYMBOLS LEGEND:
--------------
□ = Process/Action
◊ = Decision/Condition  
○ = Start/End Terminal
⬭ = Input/Output

VALIDATION RULES:
----------------
1. Card must be valid and not expired
2. PIN must be correct (max 3 attempts)
3. Withdrawal amount must be positive
4. Amount cannot exceed account balance
5. Amount cannot exceed ATM available cash
6. Amount cannot exceed daily withdrawal limit
7. Amount should be in multiples of denomination

ERROR HANDLING:
--------------
- Invalid card → Return card immediately
- Wrong PIN (3 attempts) → Block and retain card
- Insufficient funds → Show error, return to menu
- ATM out of cash → Show error, suggest other ATM
- Network error → Show error, cancel transaction

SECURITY FEATURES:
-----------------
- PIN encryption during transmission
- Transaction timeout (30 seconds)
- Card retention for suspicious activity
- Transaction logging for audit trail
